Public transport

A bit tricky, but in combination with a short walk it's absolutely doable. Here are some instructions.

Car

Limited roadsite parking is available near the venue. A parking lot is currently under construction and we'll give updates on directions at a later point. Either way, it's a short walk (max 5 mins) from the parking options to the venue.

Cab

Both taxis and Ubers are plentiful in town, so that it should not be a problem to arrive or get back home at any time of the day (or night).

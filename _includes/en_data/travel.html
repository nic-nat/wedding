How to get there?

Public transport

The venue is reachable by public transport + short walk and we will give instructions shortly.
Metros run late into the night, buses run 24/7.

Car

Limited roadsite parking is available near the venue. A parking lot is currently under construction and we'll give updates on directions at a later point. It is only a short walk from parking to the venue.

Taxi

Both cabs and Ubers are plentiful in town, so that it should not be a problem to arrive or get back home at any time of the day (or night).
